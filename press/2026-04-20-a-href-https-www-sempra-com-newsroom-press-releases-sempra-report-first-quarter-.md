SAN DIEGO, April 20, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- Sempra (NYSE: SRE) plans to release its first-quarter 2026 earnings results by 8 a.m. ET on Thursday, May 7.Senior leaders from across the company will host a conference call with a slide presentation at 12 p.m. ET on Thursday, May 7. Materials will be published prior to market open the same day.Investors, analysts and others may register to listen to the live webcast and view related materials by visiting Sempra's Investors site.
